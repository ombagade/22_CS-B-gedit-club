About Us

The GEDIT Club (General Engineering and Designing Innovative Technologies) at VIT Pune is a dynamic community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to innovation, coding, and problem-solving. Our club provides a platform for students to explore new technologies, collaborate on projects, and participate in hackathons, workshops, and coding challenges.

What We Do?

  • 🚀 Organize hands-on workshops on trending technologies like AI, Web Development, Cybersecurity, and more.
  • 💻 Conduct Hackathons & Competitions to encourage real-world problem-solving.
  • 🎓 Provide mentorship and guidance for students looking to build projects and improve their coding skills.
  • 🤝 Collaborate with industry experts & professionals to bring insightful tech talks and networking opportunities.
  • 👥 Encourage teamwork and leadership through various club activities.
